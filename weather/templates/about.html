About

MetOffice Project Automatically downloads weather data such as

  1. Maximum Temperature
  2. Minimum Temperature
  3. Average Temperature
  4. Rainfall
  5. Sunshine
on a click of a button, for the following countries
  1. UK
  2. England
  3. Scotland
  4. Wales

It also provides options to visualize data in form of charts, which will enhance analyst to compare data.
